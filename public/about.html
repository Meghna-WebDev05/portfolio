About Me

Hi! I’m Meghna, a 19-year-old student from the beautiful city of Ajmer, Rajasthan.
I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA), where I’m diving deep into the world of technology
and expanding my skills in coding and software development. I’m someone who’s naturally curious, with a love for
learning and a good sense of humor that helps me connect easily with others.

Creativity has always been a big part of who I am.
I enjoy painting, sketching, and writing—each of these hobbies gives me a different way to express myself and bring ideas to life.
Whether I’m drawing detailed artwork or crafting short stories, I find peace and excitement in the creative process.
On the flip side, I also have a strong interest in tech and logical thinking,
which is what drew me to coding and computer science.

Outside of academics and art, I love playing badminton and exploring new creative projects.
I consider myself a mix of playful and focused—always ready to learn, laugh, and try something new.
Through this website, I hope to share my work,
connect with like-minded people, and grow as both a creator and a coder.
